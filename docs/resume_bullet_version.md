# Resume bullet version

Use this version on the resume:

**Mock CAD Rates, FX, and Trading-Book Pricing Prototype | Python, Excel, VBA, SQL**

- Built a simulated front-office pricing and risk prototype for mock CAD fixed-income and FX positions, covering Government of Canada bonds, FX spot/forwards, interest-rate swaps, bond forwards, and interest-rate futures.

- Created trade-level inputs for notional, maturity, coupon, side, currency, price, yield, FX rate, forward points, and counterparty, then produced position-level valuation and exposure outputs.

- Calculated daily mark-to-market PnL, DV01, duration, convexity, FX exposure, VaR, Expected Shortfall, and risk-limit utilisation across a mock trading book.

- Built dealer-quote comparison and blotter-control checks to flag stale quotes, missing prices, duplicate trade IDs, off-market quotes, and limit breaches before report output.

- Added a simple Excel/VBA reporting layer to refresh desk-level CSV outputs for PnL, position exposure, risk summary, quote comparison, and limit breaches.
